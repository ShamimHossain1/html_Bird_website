About Me

Hello! My name is Shamim Hossain Srabon, and I am a passionate bird enthusiast and nature lover. I created this website to share my fascination with the Red-bellied Woodpecker, a remarkable species that plays a vital role in our ecosystems.

I have been observing and studying birds for over 2 years, and I am excited to provide educational resources to help others learn about these incredible creatures. Through this website, I aim to raise awareness about the importance of bird conservation and the beauty of avian life.
